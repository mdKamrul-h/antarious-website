Core Capabilities

Six ways Freya works differently from any AI you've used before.

Freya is not a feature. She is an operating layer — built to bridge the gap between people, systems, and decisions in any complex organisation.

01 · Always On
Persistent Memory

Freya remembers every decision, preference, and context from every session — permanently. Brief her once. She retains it indefinitely, building an institutional memory that outlasts any individual staff member and strengthens over time.

Compounds with every session
02 · Always Watching
Proactive Alerting

Freya doesn't wait to be asked. She monitors your live data continuously and surfaces problems before they escalate — with context, likely cause, and a draft response prepared for your review. Not just a number. An insight with a clear path to action.

Real-time, every data source
03 · Always Transparent
Transparent Reasoning

Every Freya output shows its full reasoning chain, data sources, confidence level, and timestamp. When she makes a recommendation, you see exactly why — with citations traceable to original data. Full audit trail on every single action taken by any agent.

Every action fully logged
04 · Always Controlled
Human-in-the-Loop

Freya executes within your configured boundaries. High-stakes actions require explicit human approval. Low-risk tasks run autonomously. You decide exactly what Freya can do without approval — and that boundary scales with your confidence over time. AI prepares. Humans decide and are accountable.

Configurable per action type
05 · Always Coordinated
Multi-Agent Orchestration

Freya coordinates up to 13 specialist agents simultaneously — each expert in their specific domain, all sharing the same context through a central intelligence layer. No information loss at handoffs. No coordination overhead. A complete complex workflow from a single instruction to Freya.

13 agents, zero friction
06 · Always Improving
Continuous Learning

Every edit, approval, and override teaches Freya your preferences. Every outcome improves future recommendations. Your Antarious deployment gets more accurate, more tailored, and more powerful every single day — compounding over time. The longer you use it, the better it becomes for your specific organisation.

Trust & Transparency

Built on a foundation of
accountability, not blind automation.

Every organisation that deploys Freya is trusting it with real decisions, real data, and real consequences. We built transparency, human control, and security into the architecture from the ground up — not as afterthoughts.

Human Always in Control

Every Freya output requires a human review and approval step before any external action is taken. No agent sends, submits, or triggers without a designated officer authorising it. AI prepares. Humans decide. Accountability stays with your team.

Full Audit Trail

Every action taken by every agent is logged with timestamp, data source, reasoning chain, and the human who approved it. Every decision is permanently queryable. Full transparency — not as a feature, but as a default operating principle.

Data Sovereignty

Your data is hosted in a fully isolated, dedicated environment. No data is shared with other organisations. No data is used to train external models. Full data residency documentation provided. SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant.

️
Configurable Autonomy

You configure exactly what Freya can do autonomously — granular per action type, per agent, per role, per user. Start with maximum oversight. Expand Freya's autonomy as your confidence builds. Always fully within your configured boundary.

Freya FAQs

What teams ask most about how Freya works.

These answers are adapted from the comprehensive FAQ and focus on Freya's day-to-day operation model.

What does Freya actually do?

Freya continuously monitors operations, alerts with context, executes multi-step workflows, and maintains institutional memory across decisions and outcomes.

How does the morning brief work?

A daily intelligence brief is generated at a configured time, summarising key changes, anomalies, priorities, and recommended actions for leadership.

Can teams ask Freya questions in natural language?

Yes. Freya answers from connected sources with cited analysis, while respecting each user's permission scope and data access boundaries.

What if a Freya output is incorrect, and does Freya run 24/7?

Freya runs continuously for monitoring and analysis, while external actions always require human approval. If an output is incorrect, teams can reject, revise, or correct source data before action, preserving full accountability through the approval trail.
