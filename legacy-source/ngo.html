Signature Feature

Psychometric Profiling.
How better credit decisions unlock better outcomes.

For microfinance and credit-linked development programmes, Psychometric Profiling addresses the fundamental challenge: how do you assess creditworthiness when there is no formal credit history? Antarious uses behavioural and cognitive signals to support fairer lending decisions, stronger portfolio quality, and wider access for underserved borrowers.

Three Assessment Pillars
Pillar 01
Entrepreneurial Intelligence

Problem-solving ability, adaptability, and innovative thinking. The cognitive traits that predict business resilience in challenging environments — assessed through how applicants think, not just what they know.

Pillar 02
Financial Behaviour

Savings discipline, expense management, understanding of credit obligations. Evaluates financial behaviour and discipline without requiring formal credit history or collateral documentation.

Pillar 03
Business Viability

Market awareness, customer understanding, pricing knowledge, operational planning. Tests real-world business readiness before a loan is approved — identifying applicants with genuine market viability.

The 6-Step Assessment Process
1
Digital Assessment Delivery

Applicant completes a structured assessment on any smartphone with audio support. 15–20 minutes. No facilitator required. Designed for low-literacy users in local language.

2
Multi-Dimension Scoring

AI analyses 7 psychometric dimensions simultaneously — entrepreneurial spirit, risk appetite, financial discipline, social capital, business knowledge, resilience, and motivation.

3
Portfolio Cross-Reference

Agent compares the applicant's profile against historical loan portfolio outcomes — identifying which profile types have historically performed well in similar contexts.

4
Credit Score Generation

Composite creditworthiness score with recommended loan amount, tenure, and repayment structure — tailored to the applicant's specific profile.

5
Loan Officer Review

AI output is a recommendation, not a decision. Loan officer reviews the full profile, uses AI risk flags to guide the interview, and makes the final credit decision. Human in the loop, always.

6
Outcome Learning Loop

Repayment performance feeds back to the model — every loan outcome makes the next assessment more precise. The system improves continuously as your portfolio grows.

NGO FAQs

Answers for programme and field teams.

Practical clarifications for donor reporting, partner operations, and development programme delivery.

Can Freya generate donor M&E reports from live data?

Yes. Freya drafts narrative and KPI sections from connected MIS sources, then routes outputs to officers for review and approval before submission.

How does Freya handle different partner data formats?

Field Data Analyst supports mixed submission formats and normalises them into a unified model so leadership dashboards and reports remain consistent.

Is psychometric profiling a final lending decision?

No. It provides AI-supported recommendations across seven behavioural dimensions. Loan officers remain the final decision makers in every case.

Can we detect programme risks earlier?

Yes. Freya continuously tracks partner and beneficiary signals, triggers early warnings, and includes contextual analysis to support timely intervention.
