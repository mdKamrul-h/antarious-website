Business FAQs

Answers for GTM and revenue teams.

Focused answers for business operators, SDR leaders, RevOps teams, and agencies. For full detail, see the comprehensive FAQ.

Can Freya replace our fragmented GTM stack?

Yes. Freya connects CRM, ad channels, analytics, and outreach so execution, reporting, and optimisation run in one operating layer instead of disconnected tools.

How does SDR automation work in practice?

Freya orchestrates prospecting, enrichment, personalisation, sequence scheduling, and reply classification. Human owners still approve high-impact actions based on your rules.

Does Freya support attribution across channels?

Yes. The analytics layer runs multi-model attribution across touchpoints and ties signal quality back to pipeline, CAC, and conversion outcomes for better budget allocation.

Can agencies run multiple client workspaces safely?

Yes. Agencies can isolate client environments, preserve brand context per account, and deliver automated reporting while keeping governance and approvals under agency control.
